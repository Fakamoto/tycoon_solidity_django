Tycoon Game made in Blockchain.

You need to buy a Land to start playing, it costs 1 ether. You can only have 1 land at a time.
Once you buy a Land, 100 coins are delivered to you as a gift.
Collectors mine 1 coin per second.
Collector multipliers multiply each Collector mining power by a factor of 2.
Fetch button updates the current state of your land, displaying the new data on the screen.
If you manage to have more than 100,000 coins, and 10 Collectors, then you win the game and 1 ether will be transferred to your account.
